🔀 WHAT-IF SIMULATOR
ℹ SIMULATION GUIDE

Use the What-If Simulator to evaluate alternate strategies before applying them.

Hold: Simulates the effect of holding a train at its current station for a specified duration to allow higher-priority trains to clear the section.

Reroute: Estimates extra time and distance for routing a train via an alternate path to avoid a blocked section.

Results are advisory only. Controller retains override authority.
